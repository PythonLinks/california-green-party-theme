What I have to do when update this theme?
- Update Bootstrap version at inc/classes/BootstrapBasic4.php in `enqueueScriptsAndStyles` and `registerCommonScriptsAndStyles` methods.
- Update Font Awesome version at inc/classes/BootstrapBasic4.php in `enqueueScriptsAndStyles` method.
- Update theme version number at style.css and at 'inc/classes/BootstrapBasic4.php in `enqueueScriptsAndStyles` method.
- Update changelog.md
